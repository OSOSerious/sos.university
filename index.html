About SOS.University

SOS.University is dedicated to revolutionizing education through AI technology and fostering social good. Our platform:

  • Integrates AI: Utilizes chatbots, AI agents, and language models for personalized learning.
  • Promotes Practical Skills: Focuses on applying AI to real-world scenarios across industries.
  • Encourages Innovation: Supports students and entrepreneurs in harnessing AI for social impact.
  • Bridges the Digital Divide: Coordinates technology donations to ensure equal access to education.

Our Social Impact

At SOS.University, we believe in the power of education to transform lives and communities. Our initiatives include:

  • Fostering a community of innovators committed to using AI for social good
  • Supporting local and global initiatives that align with our mission of accessible education
